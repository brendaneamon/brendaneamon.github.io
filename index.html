Mythos Hubclose

Mythos Hub constitutes the fruition of efforts that began when I was an adjunct instructor in Classics at the University of Maryland, College Park. Several years ago, I began compiling various references to mythological subjects in pop culture—music, art, comedy, theater—in order to enrich my students' appreciate of the deep impact of ancient tales on modern life.

Now, I've begun to make that my vision a reality with this app, which is built using the MEAN stack: MongoDB, Express.js, AngularJS, and Node.js. In the coming weeks, I'm going to be adding content and expanding the functionality, which currently includes login via Twitter.

ZoosnapZclose

ZoosnapZ is a Ruby on Rails web application with an AngularJS front end. The application is the result of a group effort by three developers: Brendan Magee (i.e., yours truly), Christine Movius, and Charles Hwang.

ZoosnapZ was designed to provide a mobile-friendly and user-friendly experience of the data about the various animals at the Zoo. We set out to improve on the experience currently available on the National Zoo's website by focusing exclusively on the animals themselves, rather than other zoo-related information (e.g., events, membership, etc.)

Made in Washington, DC
